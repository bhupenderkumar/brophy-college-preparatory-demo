Our Mission & History

Founded in 1928, Brophy College Preparatory has a rich history of academic excellence and community service. Our school is named after Father John Brophy, a Jesuit priest who believed in the importance of education in shaping the minds and hearts of young men.

At Brophy, we believe that education is not just about academics, but about helping students develop into compassionate, critically thinking, and creative individuals. Our faculty and staff are dedicated to creating a supportive and inclusive learning environment that fosters growth and development.

From extracurricular activities to community service projects, we offer a wide range of opportunities for students to engage with their peers and develop their interests and passions. Whether it's through sports, music, or the arts, we believe that every student has something unique to offer.
